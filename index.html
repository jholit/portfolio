About

Me, The Product Designer

Someone who cares deeply about how systems behave, how things feel, and how people actually use web applications—without needless complexity. By making interactions clearer and easier, users can move faster and enjoy their favorite applications without sacrificing power or depth. Experience matters just as much as aesthetics.

How I Work

  • Start by clarifying the problem & constraints
  • Map the flow before worrying about visuals
  • Use low-fi wireframes to explore options early
  • Refine layouts with the entire journey in mind
  • Document reasoning and trade-offs

Tools & Skills of the Trade

  • UI layout — spacing, readable structures
  • HTML (strong), CSS (developing), and foundational JavaScript front-end logic
