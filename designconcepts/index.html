Design Logic Showcase

It's not enough to know how to make look good. The "thought behind the design" is also just as important if not more. Down below demonstrates core skills and natural traits shaping my personal design philosophy in simple terms with demonstrations where applicable.

Fundamentals

Systemic Thinking

Seeing the big picture and designing solutions that work together seamlessly.

Heuristics Engineering

Using experience to create rules that guide smarter design decisions.

Cognitive UX Research

Understanding how people think to make interfaces intuitive and easy to use.

Design Systems Leadership

Creating design languages that keep products consistent and collaborative.

Data-Informed Visualization

Turning complex data into clear, understandable visuals.

Interaction & Motion Logic

Designing smooth, logical movements and transitions that delight users.

Intrinsic Skills

Constraint Crafting

Setting limits that spark creativity instead of restricting it.

Cross-domain Translation

Turning complex requirements into shared understanding between teams.

Reliability Bias Mitigation

Planning for edge cases to ensure the interface behaves consistently.

Heuristic Intuition

Spotting patterns quickly to predict user friction and improve experiences.
